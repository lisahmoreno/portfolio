About

Product leader with founder experience across travel, marketplaces, and B2B platforms. Currently Head of Product (B2B) at GetAway Group, the DACH leader for short-trip bookings. Previously co-founded Kietzee, a family platform that grew to serve thousands of families monthly and secured grant funding from Google.org.

I'm drawn to operationally complex products — the workflows people use every day, the partner experiences that scale badly, and the data quality problems hiding underneath. Particularly interested in where AI can remove friction from those systems without creating new ones.

The projects here are personal experiments: some connected to problems I've encountered at work, some just things I wanted to exist.
